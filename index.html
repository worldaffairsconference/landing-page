Inspiring Topics

Every year, the World Affairs Conference touches on pressing issues that affect both delegates individually and the world as a whole. This year's theme for the World Affairs Conference is Paradigm Shift: we'll be examining how ideas have fundamentally changed with the advent of new technology, changing political structures, and a declining Earth.

Engaging Speakers

The World Affairs Conference has a diverse roster of past speakers spanning both the globe and a variety of topics. Notable previous speakers include Edward Snowden, Marc Garneau, and most recently Dr. Aubrey de Grey. We'll be releasing more information about our speakers as we near the conference.

Approachable Staff

At the World Affairs Conference, our staff is dedicated to creating an amazing conference experience for all delegates. If you have any questions about the conference, don't hesistate to contact us at wac@ucc.on.ca, or through social media.
